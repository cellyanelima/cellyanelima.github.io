    Cultural Blog - Introducing Te Whare Tapa Whā

    With 4 walls, the wharenui is a symbol of these 4 dimensions. The wharenui has connections to the whenua (land, sea, natural world) which forms the foundations for the four dimensions

    My wellbeing plan using Tā Mason Durie’s Te Whare Tapa Whā model
    Wall Activities
    Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing) 40 minutes on the treadmill
    Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing) Having breakfast and having dinner with my family every evening
    Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing) Meditation before class
    Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing) Praying before sleep
    Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment) Enjoy the night sky once a week / I drink my coffee while enjoying what is happening around me with the beautiful view from my house.
